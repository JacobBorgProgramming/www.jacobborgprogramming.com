Hello! My name is Jacob.

I'm currently studying for a degree in digital design with an emphasis animation at Grand Canyon University, but my creative journey did not start here.

I have also been interested in art, but in junior high I got serious about it when my sister got Cheetah 3D, a 3d animation and modeling suite. Being a great sister, she let me use the application on her computer, and I fell in love with animation. Since then, I have studied animation, practiced with several animation systems (including Blender 3D and Maya), interned in 3D animation, produced two animated short films, and free lanced my design services.
